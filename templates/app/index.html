What causes my speed to fluctuate ?

For a variety of response, speeds can change during the day. You may learn more about how to improve

These are some of the reasons:

  • Interference with Wi-Fi (from outside your home as well as from your own devices)
  • Update to your device, software and download( Mobile devices and TV's as well as computers )
  • Others on the same ISP exchange are using the internet more.
  • Problem wth your internet service provider that are only temporary.
  • Programs that are currently running on your PC ( including browser extensions )
  • Having issues with your router, cable tat are faulty
